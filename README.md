# 🏛️ Smart Municipal Hierarchy Management System

A full-stack web application developed for **Nagar Nigam (Municipal Corporation)** to digitally manage departmental hierarchies, officers, zones, wards, and reporting structures through a centralized administration portal.

The system replaces manual hierarchy management with a dynamic, scalable, and multilingual solution that supports different organizational structures for different departments.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🏢 Department Management
- 👨‍💼 Officer Management
- 🔄 Dynamic Multi-Level Hierarchy (L1, L2, L3...)
- 🌐 Hindi & English Language Support
- 📝 Dynamic Level Names
- 📍 Zone Management
- 🗺️ Ward Management
- 🔗 Reports-To Hierarchy
- 👥 Role-Based Access (Admin & Operator)
- 📊 Dynamic Hierarchy Reports
- 📄 PDF Export
- 📑 Excel Export
- ✅ Unique Ward Assignment Validation
- 🎯 Automatic Zone-wise Officer Mapping
- 📱 Responsive Government Dashboard

## 📌 Key Functionalities

### Department Management
- Create and manage multiple municipal departments.
- Configure department-wise officer hierarchy.

### Officer Management
- Add, edit and manage officers.
- Assign reporting hierarchy.
- Manage dynamic officer levels.
- Support bilingual level names.

### Zone & Ward Management
- Assign officers to zones and wards.
- Prevent duplicate ward assignment within the same department.
- Support departments with or without zones and wards.

### Dynamic Reports
- Automatically generate hierarchy reports.
- Dynamic columns based on department configuration.
- Export reports to PDF and Excel.
- Support Hindi and English.

### Role-Based System
- Admin Portal
- Operator Portal
